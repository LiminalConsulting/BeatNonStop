### 2026-04-12 · Event concept
**Decision**: "Lua Nova" — celebrate actual new moon of May 16. Cosmic / celestial aesthetic. Not explicitly spiritual on public-facing materials, but spiritual/ritual framing guides internal decisions.
**Rationale**: Genuine coincidence with astronomical new moon. Theme that hasn't been done locally.
**Implication**: Decor + marketing lean celestial. Spiritual/manifestation language reserved for community context.

### 2026-04-12 · Age policy: 13+ with wristband tiering
**Decision**: Admission from age 13. White wristband for 13–17 (no alcohol). Adult wristband for 18+. Under-13 not admitted, no exceptions.
**Rationale**: Families with teens are normal in Vila Verde culture. 18+ would cut real attendees. Wristband system protects parish alcohol license while respecting local culture.
**Implication**: Door team must ID-check anyone who looks under 25. Bar staff briefed: "no wristband or white wristband = no alcohol, no exceptions." Parent consent form for 13–17. Free soft drink for 13–17.

### 2026-04-12 · Artist payment structure
**Decision**: Cash, no contracts. 3 artists paid (Sun Profile €300, Kika Lewis €400, John €750). 4 artists unpaid (break even via food/logistics/exposure). 50% upfront. €100 success bonus to 6 non-Tiago artists if event succeeds.
**Rationale**: Friend-based network. Tiago's standard practice. Contracts would add bureaucracy incompatible with this culture.
**Implication**: Daily contact with artists is the substitute for legal enforceability. Trust is the mechanism.

### 2026-04-12 · Launch day = Tuesday Apr 14
**Decision**: Tickets go live Tuesday, not Monday.
**Rationale**: 24h buffer for Stripe verification. Tuesday launch pairs with first artist reveal for a sharper marketing moment.
**Implication**: Monday is setup day (domain, Shotgun setup, final QA). Tuesday is public launch.
